An adverb is a word that modifies a [verb](Verb.md) (*he sings* ***loudly***), an [adjective](Adjective.md) (***very*** *tall*), another [adverb](Adverb.md) or even a whole sentence (***fortunately***, *I had brought an umbrella*).
